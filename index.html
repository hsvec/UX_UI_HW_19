Hey! I'm Hannah Svec.

I’m a UX/UI Designer based in Salt Lake City, UT and I’m passionate about bringing concepts to life to spark joy in everyday interactions. I design experiences to simplify and build a better world with a passion for emphasizing the natural beauty that surrounds us and that which we create. With a background and B.A. in Psychology from Macalester College, I specialize in thoughtful human-centered design and writing, and enjoy solving user problems with a deeply empathetic approach.

My UX Skills

User Research

User research is typically conducted through personal interviews in order to get to know our users needs in a meaningful way.

User Empathy

User empathy is executed most effectively through the use of empathy maps, storyboards and user journey maps, in order to gain a greater understanding of our user needs.

Information Architecture

The organization of information is pivotal to the effectiveness of our products, and I often use collaborative card sorting to create a strong map of sites and applications.
